What We Do

Girls Tech Club creates opportunities for girls and young women to learn, build, lead, and participate meaningfully in the digital economy.

Digital Skills Training

We provide practical training in digital literacy, web development, design, productivity tools, and other tech-focused skills.

Mentorship & Leadership

We connect girls with mentors, role models, and leadership opportunities that help them build confidence and pursue their goals.

Innovation & Entrepreneurship

We encourage problem-solving, creativity, and entrepreneurship so girls can turn ideas into impactful solutions and future careers.

Building a Future Where Girls Belong in Tech

Girls Tech Club is committed to expanding access to technology education and creating spaces where girls can learn, grow, and lead. Through programs, mentorship, and partnerships, we help bridge the gender gap in technology.

Our work supports girls and young women to gain relevant skills, discover opportunities, and contribute meaningfully to their communities and the digital future.
