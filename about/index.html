About Girls Tech Club

Who we are, what we stand for, and why our work matters.

Our Story

Girls Tech Club is a nonprofit initiative focused on empowering girls and young women with digital, leadership, and innovation skills. We believe technology can be a powerful tool for inclusion, opportunity, and long-term economic empowerment.

Through training programs, mentorship, community engagement, and partnerships, we work to ensure girls are not left behind in the digital future.

Our Mission

To empower girls and young women through technology education, digital literacy, mentorship, innovation, and leadership opportunities that prepare them for the future.

Our Vision

A future where every girl has the confidence, skills, support, and access needed to thrive in technology, leadership, entrepreneurship, and the digital economy.

Our Core Values

Inclusion

We believe every girl deserves access to opportunities, tools, and spaces to learn and grow.

Entrepreneurship

We equip girls with practical skills, confidence, and support to shape their own futures.

Innovation

We encourage creativity, problem-solving, and the use of technology to address real challenges.

Leadership

We nurture leadership potential so girls can become changemakers in their communities.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnerships, community, and mentorship to create lasting impact.

Opportunity

We open pathways for girls to pursue education, entrepreneurship, and careers in tech.
