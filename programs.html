Our Programs

Training, mentorship, leadership, and innovation opportunities for girls and young women.

Basic ICT & Digital Literacy

Introductory technology training that helps girls build confidence in using computers, the internet, digital tools, and online resources.

Creative Tech & Web Skills

Hands-on learning in design, digital creativity, and web development to expose girls to practical and marketable tech skills.

Leadership & Mentorship

Sessions that connect girls with mentors, strengthen communication skills, and encourage leadership, confidence, and goal setting.

Entrepreneurship Training

Programs that introduce girls to business thinking, innovation, and practical entrepreneurship skills for future economic empowerment.

Career Exposure & Guidance

Activities that expose girls to career paths in technology and related industries, while helping them understand the skills and pathways required.

Community Outreach

Awareness, advocacy, and community engagement efforts that promote girls’ participation in education, digital inclusion, and technology opportunities.
